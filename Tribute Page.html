Here's a time line of the World War II

  • January 30, 1933 - Adolf Hitler becomes chancellor of Germany and later assumes dictatorial powers.
  • September 1,1939 - Germany invades Poland, inciting their allies Britain and France to declare war.
  • September 17, 1939 - Soviet leader Joseph Stalin orders the invasion of Poland, securing a share of Polish territory.
  • June 14, 1940 - Paris falls to German forces.
  • December 7, 1941 - Japan attacks Pearl Harbor, starting war with te US. Hitler declares war on America 4 days later.
